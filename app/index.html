How to Play

Clear your Hold before your rivals. After the chosen number of rounds, the lowest total score wins.

  1. Play adjacent cards onto the four Runs. Empty Runs begin with an Ace or Queen; an unset opening card can establish either direction.
  2. Kings are wild for values 2 through Jack. They may continue or reverse a set Run, or establish direction after one natural opening card.
  3. Play as many legal cards as you can. Emptying your Hand refills it to five cards.
  4. End your turn by discarding one Hand card to a Port. Port top cards can be played on later turns.
  5. Completed Runs send their Kings to the Brig. A qualifying Key King springs every imprisoned King for a Jailbreak.
  6. During a Jailbreak, play every released King before discarding—or the remaining Kings are reshuffled into the draw deck and add penalties to your Hold.
